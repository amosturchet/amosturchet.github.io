Research


Main Research Interests:

Diophantine, Arithmetic and Algebraic Geometry, Number Theory.

Research Description :

My main research interests lies in the intersection between Number Theory and Algebraic Geometry, in the study of the distribution of rational and integral solutions to polynomial equations; equivalently - in a more fancier way - I study integral and rational points on algebraic varieties defined over number fields or function fields.
I spent my Ph.D. focusing on algebraic hyperbolicity for complements of normal crossing divisors in the projective plane using both standard techniques from arithmetic over function fields and new ideas coming from Logarithmic Geometry and Moduli Spaces of log-stable maps. I recently got interested in application of Vojta's Conjecture to Uniformity Problems.
