Research


Main Research Interests:

Diophantine Geometry, Arithmetic and Algebraic Geometry.

Research Descritpion :

I began focusing on Lang-Vojta Conjecture in my Master Thesis and I kept working on this intriguing open problem in my Ph.D. In particular I studied algebraic hyperbolicity for complements of normal crossing divisors in the projective plane using both standard techniques from arithmetic over function fields and new ideas coming from Logarithmic Geometry and Moduli Spaces of log-stable maps. I recently got interested in application of Vojta's Conjecture to Uniformity Problems.
