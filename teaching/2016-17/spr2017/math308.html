Course Description:

Math 308 is an introductory course in linear algebra intended for students in engineering, mathematics, and the sciences. Topics will include systems of linear equations, vector spaces, matrices, subspaces, orthogonality, eigenvalues, eigenvectors, and applications. Here is a tentative schedule.

Textbook:

  • Main Textbook
    Linear Algebra with Applications by Jeffrey Holt. You may purchase a hard copy of the text at the bookstore or use the electronic version that comes with WebAssign.
  • Further Readings
