Introductory Material

In the course we are not assuming that the participants had some previous contacts with Algebraic Geometry. However, since the course could have, especially in the beginning, an abstract feeling, we think that some geometric intuition could help (any other experience with manifolds from the complex-analytic point of view will be as well useful). Hence we collect here some material that could be useful for those participants without any previous experience of Algebraic Geometry.
  • MMA320
    This is the page for the course "Introduction to Algebraic Geometry" given by Jan Stevens. It contains very well written notes that can be useful to get in touch with the constructions of classical Algebraic Geometry. It can also be very helpful to build up some geometric intuition on curves and (partially) surfaces.
  • A pre-introduction to algebraic geometry by pictures
    This page written by Donu Arapura is a beautiful and short presentation of some features of algebraic curves, which are doubtfully one of the more fundamental objects in Algebraic Geometry. It focuses on the geometric intuition and helps the reader's visualisation with the help of nice pictures. If you never encounter algebraic curves and want to get a quick but very intuitive idea this is one of the best starting points.
    Donu Arapura has also notes on Basic Algebraic Geometry, available here.
  • Algebraic Curves
    The classic book by Fulton on Algebraic Curves which served an introduction to Algebraic Geometry since 1969. The new TeXed version is freely available for download from the authors webpage.
  • Basic Algebraic Geometry
    A video course by Miles Reid on Basic Algebraic geometry, based on his book with the same title. The lectures, given at Sogang University, assumed no previuos knowledge of Aglebraic Geometry. These videos represents a really good opportunity to learn the basic from one of the leading figures in the classification of algebraic varities.

Other Bibliographic suggestions:

  • Reid, Miles: Undergraduate algebraic geometry. London Mathematical Society Student Texts, 12. Cambridge University Press, Cambridge, 1988.
  • Hassett, Brendan: Introduction to algebraic geometry. Cambridge University Press, Cambridge, 2007.
  • Kirwan, Frances: Complex algebraic curves. London Mathematical Society Student Texts, 23. Cambridge University Press, Cambridge, 1992.
  • Hulek, Klaus: Elementary algebraic geometry. Student Mathematical Library, 20. American Mathematical Society, Providence, RI, 2003.
  • Miranda, Rick: Algebraic curves and Riemann surfaces. Graduate Studies in Mathematics, 5. American Mathematical Society, Providence, RI, 1995.
