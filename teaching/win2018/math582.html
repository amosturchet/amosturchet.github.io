Course Description:

Math 582 is a graduate course in Arithmetic Geometry intended for graduate students in pure mathematics. The main focus of the course will be two fundamental results that describe the distribution of rational points on curves: the Mordell-Weil Theorem and Faltings Theorem.

Textbook:

  • Main Textbook
    Diophantine Geometry - An Introduction by Mark Hindry and Joseph Silverman.
  • Further Readings
    • Heights in Diophantine Geometry - Enrico Bombieri and Walter Gubler;
    • Integral Points on Algebraic Varieties: An Introduction to Diophantine Geometry - Pietro Corvaja;
    • Arithmetic of Elliptic Curves - Joseph Silverman;
