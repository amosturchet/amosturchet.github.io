Course Description:

Math 402 is an introductory course in abstract algebra that focuses on the elementary theory of Groups. Topics will include: Cosets and Lagrange's theorem. Homomorphisms, normal subgroups, quotient groups, and the fundamental isomorphism theorems. Cyclic and symmetric groups. Orders and Cauchy's theorem. Direct products. Automorphisms.
