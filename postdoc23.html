Postdoc position in Number Theory and Arithmetic Geometry

Department of Mathematics and Physics - Roma Tre University

Basic Information

  • The post-doc position is in Number Theory, with special emphasis on Diophantine and Arithmetic Geometry.
  • Applicants are expected to interact actively with the members of the Number Theory group of Roma Tre and to participate in the proposed activities, such as seminars, working groups and PhD courses.
  • The position is funded by the PRIN 2022 "Semiabelian varieties, Galois representations and related Diophantine problems" for 1 year.
  • Candidates should have a Ph.D. degree in mathematics.
  • The expected starting date of the position is in February 2024.
  • The annual gross salary is 32000 euros, (after taxes, this corresponds approximately to 1.950 euros per month, which is a reasonable salary to afford the living costs in Rome). The candidate can also count on some funding for traveling or inviting people at the department.
  • The examination process will consist of two steps: an evaluation of the CV/List of publications/Research project and, for the selected candidates, an oral interview (possibly via Skype).
  • Deadline for application: 22 December 2023.

How to apply

You can find complete information regarding the application procedure in the official call for the position (here is a short English version of it): we strongly recommend to read it carefully! In short, you should send the following documents by ordinary e-mail to the address amm.matematicafisica@uniroma3.it attaching a copy of a valid identity document:

  • Filled in forms Annex A, Annex B, and Annex C, that you can find at the end of the official call, or in the English version of it.
  • A copy (in English or Italian) of a certificate of your PhD degree.
  • A copy of an identity document.
  • CV.
  • List of publications and preprints.
  • Research project.
  • Up to 2 reccomendation letters can be sent directly from the referees to the address fabrizio.barroero@uniroma3.it.

For any further question regarding the application procedure you can contact fabrizio.barroero@uniroma3.it.
