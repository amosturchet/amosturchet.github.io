Aug 24 - Sept 4 2015: Marco Maculan (Jussieu - Paris 6)

GIT and Diophantine Approximation
Abstract: During August and September 2015, Marco Maculan (Institut Mathématiques de Jussieu - Paris VI) will give a series of lectures on Geometric Invariant Theory and Diophantine Approximation. The lectures will begin with a basic introduction to Geometric Invariant Theory (GIT) - a theory developed by Mumford in the '60s to construct moduli spaces - and then will expound a proof of Roth's Theorem - a classical result in Diophantine Approximation - based on techniques of GIT.
